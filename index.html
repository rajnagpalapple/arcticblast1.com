Peppermint Oil

**Peppermint Oil**: Extracted from the peppermint plant, this oil has been used for centuries for its medicinal properties. Beyond its refreshing aroma, peppermint oil offers analgesic, antispasmodic, and anti-inflammatory benefits, making it effective in alleviating various types of pain.

Emu Oil

**Emu Oil**: Sourced from the fat of the emu bird, emu oil is rich in Omega fatty acids. It has been recognized for its anti-inflammatory properties, making it effective in reducing swelling and pain, especially in conditions like arthritis.
4

Camphor Oil

**Camphor Oil**: Derived from the camphor tree, this oil is commonly found in topical analgesics and pain medications. It interacts with sensory nerve receptors to alleviate pain and muscle aches while stimulating blood circulation.
5

St. John’s Wort Oil

**St. John’s Wort Oil**: Extracted from the flowering tops of the Hypericum Perfuctum plant, St. John's Wort oil has a range of therapeutic properties. It acts as a muscle relaxant, nerve tonic, and anti-inflammatory agent, making it versatile in treating various pain-related conditions.
6

Menthol

**Menthol**: A natural compound found in mint plants, menthol provides a cooling sensation when applied to the skin. This cooling effect helps numb the area, providing immediate relief from pain and discomfort.
7

Camphor

**Camphor**: Similar to menthol, camphor offers a cooling effect when applied topically. It's known to alleviate pain and inflammation, making it a common ingredient in many pain-relief formulations.
8

Limonene

**Limonene**: A compound found in citrus fruit peels, limonene has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. It aids in reducing pain and inflammation, enhancing the overall effectiveness of the supplement.
9

Carvacrol

**Carvacrol**: Found in oregano oil, carvacrol has potent anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It plays a role in alleviating pain and reducing inflammation in affected areas.
10

Tocopherol

**Tocopherol**: Commonly known as Vitamin E, tocopherol has antioxidant properties that help protect cells from damage. It also aids in skin healing, making it beneficial in topical pain relief formulations.
